About Midwest Healthcare Staffing Agency

Headquartered in the Midwest United States, we build long-term partnerships between healthcare providers and highly qualified international healthcare professionals. Our integrated model combines global recruitment, immigration and visa support, credentialing, training, onboarding, and retention.

Mission

To strengthen American healthcare systems by connecting them with compassionate, compliant, world-class talent.
